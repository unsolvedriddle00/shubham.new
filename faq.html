What is silica sand used for?

Silica sand is a versatile industrial mineral used in various applications including glass manufacturing (the primary use), construction industry for concrete and mortar, water filtration systems, foundry work for metal casting, hydraulic fracturing in oil and gas industry, and sports surfaces like golf courses and volleyball courts. Our high-quality silica sand meets industry standards for all these applications.

How is silica sand extracted and processed?

Our silica sand is extracted through carefully planned quarrying operations in Sankargarh. The raw material undergoes systematic processing including washing, screening, and grading to remove impurities like clay, silt, and organic matter. We use advanced equipment to ensure consistent quality and meet specific customer requirements for different grain sizes and purity levels.

Do you offer custom sand products?

Yes, we specialize in customizing our silica sand products to meet your specific requirements. We can adjust grain size distribution, chemical composition, and other parameters based on your application needs. Our technical team works closely with clients to develop tailored solutions for specialized industrial applications.

What quality standards do you maintain?

We maintain strict quality control measures throughout our production process. Our silica sand products are tested for chemical composition, grain size distribution, and physical properties. We follow industry standards and provide detailed certificates of analysis with each shipment to ensure consistent quality that meets your specifications.

What are your delivery and shipping options?

We offer flexible delivery options including bulk truck loads, railway wagons, and container shipments. Our strategic location in Prayagraj provides excellent connectivity to major industrial centers across India. We work with reliable logistics partners to ensure timely delivery and can accommodate both small and large volume orders.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantities vary depending on the product specifications and delivery location. For standard grades, we typically accept orders from 10 tons onwards. However, we're flexible and can discuss smaller quantities for sampling or trial purposes. Contact our sales team for specific MOQ requirements for your needs.

How do you determine pricing?

Our pricing is based on several factors including product specifications, order quantity, delivery location, and current market conditions. We offer competitive rates and volume discounts for large orders. Please contact us with your specific requirements for a detailed quotation. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

What environmental measures do you follow?

We are committed to sustainable mining practices and environmental protection. Our operations include dust control measures, water recycling systems, land reclamation programs, and compliance with all environmental regulations. We regularly monitor our environmental impact and implement best practices to minimize our ecological footprint.
